------------------- MAIN.PY -------------------
This program will read and print the top news from a specific
newspaper ( or of a specific country ) on console along with the URL of 
entire report. One can copy the URL to read the entire news.

Options have been provided as per you want to hear the headlines
only or want a small description also.

--------------------- NEWS_CATAGORIES.PY --------------------
It gives you choice of catagories of the news you want to hear. Once chosen,
news will come from worldwide.
